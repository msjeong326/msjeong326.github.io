About Me

Molecular Biologist & Microbiome Researcher

Leveraging multi-omics data analysis and machine learning to investigate host resilience against pathogens and environmental microbiome dynamics.

Highly motivated and productive molecular biologist with a Ph.D. in Applied Biosciences and extensive experience in plant-microbe interactions. My expertise centers on leveraging multi-omics data analysis and machine learning to investigate host resilience against pathogens and environmental microbiome dynamics.

As a Postdoctoral Researcher at Kyungpook National University's Next-Generation Sequencing Core Research Support Center, supported by the prestigious Sejong Science Fellowship from the National Research Foundation of Korea, I lead independent research programs combining gnotobiotic systems, untargeted metabolomics, WMS (shotgun) profiling, RNA-seq, and ddPCR to dissect the mechanisms underlying host-microbe interactions.

My publication record includes 18+ peer-reviewed papers with co-authorship on Nature Communications (IF 16.6), npj Biofilms and Microbiomes (IF 9.2), and Comprehensive Reviews in Food Science and Food Safety (IF 12.0). I have presented at international conferences including ASM Microbe 2023 and 2025, and received the Grand Prize for Oral Presentation at the KMB International Conference 2024.

Research Interests

Areas of Inquiry

My research spans host-microbe interaction, environmental microbiome dynamics, and computational biology.

01
🌱

Host–Microbe Interaction

Investigating how host genotypes modulate microbiome assembly and how microbial communities mediate plant disease suppression, using gnotobiotic systems and multi-omics integration to dissect these complex interactions.

Gnotobiotics Disease Suppression Phyllosphere Rhizosphere
02
🧬

Multi-omics & Untargeted Metabolomics

Integrating WMS (shotgun) profiling, RNA-seq, untargeted metabolomics, ddPCR, and amplicon sequencing for holistic characterization of microbiome function and host-microbe metabolic crosstalk.

WMS Profiling RNA-seq Metabolomics ddPCR 16S/ITS
03
🤖

Machine Learning for Biology

Applying machine learning and deep learning approaches to biological data analysis, including object detection (YOLOv8) for crop phenotyping, and predictive models for microbial community dynamics.

Deep Learning YOLOv8 XGBoost SHAP PyTorch
04
🌊

Environmental Microbiome Dynamics

Understanding how environmental perturbations such as cyanobacterial blooms, antimicrobial resistance gene dissemination, and soil management practices reshape microbial communities in aquatic and terrestrial systems.

ARG Dynamics Cyanobacterial Blooms Soil Microbiome Community Assembly
05
📈

Bioinformatics & Computational Ecology

Developing and applying bioinformatics pipelines for metagenome assembly, MAG binning, functional annotation, and ecological statistical frameworks including null models, variance partitioning, and network analysis.

MAG Binning eggNOG Network Analysis PERMANOVA
