About

About Me

I grow cucumbers in germ-free systems to track what individual microbes actually do, and I analyze river metagenomes to understand how antimicrobial resistance genes spread across the Nakdong River watershed. I'm also studying how cyanobacterial blooms reshape the lettuce microbiome.

I handle the entire research pipeline myself — from experimental design and sequencing to statistical modeling and analysis.
